You can insert data to tables here. Please input fill in all the fields to successfully insert new data.

Insert to Content

Inserting a new content requires the following fields formatted as follows:

  • ShowID: ID that can uniquely define a content. Should be formatted as 'sxxx', where a lower-case letter 's' is followed by positive integer greater than 1
  • Type of content: Type of content should be either Movie or TV Shows.
  • Title: Title of content, can contain white space, numbers, or alphabets.
  • Released_country: Country where the content was released. First letter of the country should be upper-case: United States
  • Date aded to Netflix: Date when content was added to Netflix. Should be formatted as yyyy-mm-dd hh:mm:ss
  • Rating: Choose one of the drop down menu options for Content Rating
  • Duration: For movie, duration should be formatted as 'xx min'. For TV Show, duration should be formatted as 'xx Seasons'










Insert to Covid

Inserting a new covid record requires the following fields formatted as follows:

  • Country: Country where the record was collected fromm. First letter of the country should be upper-case: United States
  • Date recorded: Date when the record was collected. Should be formatted as yyyy-mm-dd hh:mm:ss
  • total_case: total confirmed COVID cases as of record date. Should be a positive integer.
  • new_case: new confirmed COVID cases collected on the day of record. Should be a positive integer.
  • new_deaths_per_million: New deaths attributed to COVID-19 per 1,000,000 people. Counts can include probable deaths, where reported. Should be up to decimal 2 places, ranging from 0.00 to less than 1.00







Insert to Financial

Inserting a new financial record requires the following fields formatted as follows:

  • filing_quarter_id: Date when the financial record was collected in one of the four quarters (03-31, 06-30, 09-30, 12-31). Should be formatted as yyyy-mm-dd hh:mm:ss
  • revenue: Positive decimal up to 2 decimal places
  • rd_expense: Positive decimal up to 2 decimal places
  • net_income: Positive decimal up to 2 decimal places
  • earnings_per_share: Positive decimal up to 4 decimal places
